🔍 Introduction to AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to mimic human intelligence. It enables computers to learn, reason, and make decisions.

⚙️ How Does AI Work?

AI works by analyzing data, recognizing patterns, and making predictions. Key techniques include:

  • 🔹 Machine Learning: AI learns from data.
  • 🔹 Neural Networks: Mimics the human brain.
  • 🔹 Natural Language Processing: AI understands human language.

🚀 Why is AI Important?

AI is transforming industries like healthcare, finance, and entertainment by improving efficiency, accuracy, and automation.
